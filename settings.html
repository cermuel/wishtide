❓What is Wishtide?

Wishtide is a social wishlist and gifting app that makes celebrations easier. You can create wishlists for birthdays, events, or causes, share them with friends, and contribute to others' wishes—all in one place.

🎁 How do I create a wishlist?

Creating a wishlist is simple! Just tap "Create Wishlist", add the items or experiences you'd like, set privacy preferences (public, friends, or private), and share it with your circle.

💸 How do contributions work?

Friends and supporters can contribute directly to your wishlist items or event fund using secure payment options like Stripe or PayPal. You'll be able to track contributions in real time, and contributors can choose to stay anonymous or be recognized.

🔒 Can I trust the platform?

Absolutely. Wishtide uses trusted payment providers (Stripe and PayPal) to handle all transactions securely. You're always in control of your privacy settings, and you can decide who sees your wishlists and activities.
